Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is BHARAT की LIBRARY ?

    BHARAT की LIBRARY is a library which provides government services available to citizens electronically by online infrastructure improvement and also by enhancing internet connectivity.

  • So, how can I upload my documents?

    You just need to go the library section of the wesite and just fill your details and there you can easily upload your all the documents.

  • How can I submit my Supporting documents in case of online Self Service Update portal (SSUP)?

    You will be required to upload original scanned (with color scanner) copies of PoA document as per the Valid Documents List.
